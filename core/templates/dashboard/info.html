Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure with the AI system?

Yes, all student data is encrypted and handled in compliance with educational data privacy regulations. The AI only accesses information necessary to provide academic assistance.

Can the AI help with specific course content?

Absolutely! The AI is trained on your course materials and can provide explanations, examples, and guidance specific to your curriculum.

How does the AI generate my timetable?

The timetable generator considers your enrolled courses, preferred study times, breaks between classes, and optimal learning patterns to create an efficient schedule.

Is there a cost to use these AI features?

No, the AI-powered assistance is provided as part of the university's commitment to enhancing student learning experiences at no additional cost.
